Welcome

I'm Andrew DeFrank, a DC native and Dupont Circle resident from 2024 to 2026. I served as ANC commissioner for ANC 2B01 from June 2025 until my resignation in 2026, after stepping in to fill a vacancy a few months into the two-year term. As commissioner, it was my responsibility to answer questions, help solve problems, and advocate for my constituents in 2B01. I resigned after moving out of the District before the end of the term.

This site is preserved as an archive. Use the links below to learn more about my background, ANCs, the district, and the actions I took as commissioner.
